About Us

ITS Material Advantage Chapter (IMAC) is the first and only Material Advantage Chapter in Indonesia that was established in 17th December 2017. We are a student organization that aims to spread and develop the knowledge about minerals, materials, and metallurgy for ITS Students which are the fields that we are focused on.
